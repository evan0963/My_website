Why I Chose UMD

The lake, the school and the nature.

I chose UMD, the second school I applied for, for a few reasons

  • Beautiful environment around me
  • Small class sizes with professors who actually know you
  • Career connections through LSBE and UMD networking events

What I Love About Duluth

A city built for students who love nature, adventure, and community.

Duluth has become home to me, its mix of outdoor activities, friendly people, and scenic views make it a great place to live and study.

My favorite parts of Duluth:

  • Chester Bowl & Spirit Mountain in winter
  • Hartley Trail
  • Asian Kitchen
  • Lincoln Park

My Campus Experience

How UMD has shaped my time here.

Being a student at UMD has given me opportunities to grow academically, socially, and personally.

Some highlights of my UMD experience so far:

  • Being an active member of my service fraternity, Alpha Delta Nu Mu
  • Being a data analyst intern for the career center
  • Being the Vice President of Black Men Serving Excellence
  • Exploring Duluth with friends between exams and projects

Overall, UMD has become a place that balances learning, community, and adventure — and I’m glad I chose to study here.
